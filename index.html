You are not alone - we are here! Glide and spin with your friends across the evening sky in the world of We Are Here. Inspired by the concept of a nonverbal chatroom, We Are Here's colorful art, calming music, and simple controls provide a safe sandbox environment for players to unwind, dance, and express themselves.

We Are Here is coded in JavaScript and uses Firebase to synchronize players anywhere in the world in a shared game space. #weareheregame
